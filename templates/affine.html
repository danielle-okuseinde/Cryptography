The Affine Cipher

This is the Affine Cipher. It is a monoalphabetic substitution cipher that uses the formula E(x) = (ax + b)%26, where a is an integer relatively prime to 26, b is an integer, and x is the letter value. The alphabet is numbered starting from 0 (A=0, Z=25).
