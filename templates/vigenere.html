The Vigenere Cipher

The vigenere cipher is an interesting cipher where a keyword is used to encrypt and decrypt a message. You map each letter of the keyword to a letter in the plaintext/ciphertext, and you repeat the keyword until there are no letters left. Then, you shift each letter in the plaintext/ciphertext by the value of its corresponding keyword letter.
